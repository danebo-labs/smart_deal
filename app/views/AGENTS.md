# View Rules

## Hotwire And Tailwind

* Optimize for mobile-first responsive UX.
* Prefer Turbo Frames and Turbo Streams over SPA-like patterns.
* Use Tailwind consistently with existing views.
* Avoid unnecessary frontend state.
* Keep perceived responsiveness high.

## Technician UX

Primary users are field elevator technicians working with:

* low light
* gloves
* unreliable connectivity
* small mobile screens

Prioritize:

* minimal typing
* high clarity
* low interaction friction
* large tap targets
* concise UI
* semantic names and aliases over technical filenames
